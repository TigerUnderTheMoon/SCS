# Cover Letter Draft

Date: 2026-06-03

To: Editors, *Sustainability*

Manuscript title: Smart City Construction, Sustainable Urban Transition, and Carbon Emissions: Nonlinear Evidence from Chinese Cities

Dear Editors,

We submit our manuscript for consideration in *Sustainability*. The paper studies whether multidimensional smart-city coordination reduces urban CO2 emissions in China. Using an observed panel of 284 Chinese prefecture-level and above cities from 2006 to 2021, we construct a spatial coupling coordination degree index that extends the production-living-ecological framework with a digital dimension.

The main result is nonlinear. The estimated association between coordination and log carbon emissions is inverted U-shaped, with a turning point inside the observed SCCD range. The paper also shows that industrial upgrading and green technological innovation are associated restructuring pathways, and that digital economy development weakens the marginal emission cost of coordination.

This manuscript is original, is not under consideration elsewhere, and all authors have approved the submission. There are no conflicts of interest to declare.
