## 第2阶段：Abstract和Introduction重写 [设计完成，需全面应用]

### Abstract完整新版本

**新Abstract：**

Cities are increasingly governed through digital networks as well as physical infrastructure, but the environmental implications of coordination are not automatically positive. This study incorporates digital space as a functional dimension alongside production, living, and ecological functions, and examines how multidimensional spatial coordination affects carbon emissions in 284 Chinese cities from 2006 to 2024. The results reveal an inverted U-shaped relationship—a coordination paradox. While coordination reduces information frictions and improves allocation efficiency, early-stage coordination may simultaneously intensify infrastructure expansion, reorganization, and energy demand. Most cities remain below the turning point (0.542), indicating that the efficiency-oriented stage is not yet dominant. Restructuring evidence suggests that industrial upgrading and green innovation function as stage-dependent pathways. Digital economy development attenuates marginal emission costs and flattens the nonlinear pattern. The core finding is a governance tradeoff: coordination can only support sustainable transition when data-enabled governance shifts cities from expansion-oriented integration toward efficiency-oriented operation.

Keywords: Urban multidimensional coordination; Digital space; Carbon emissions; Industrial restructuring; Governance sequencing; Data-enabled governance

### Introduction关键段落重写

【第1段末】加入：
"Against this backdrop, a fundamental question emerges: does urban coordination support low-carbon transition, or does it simply accelerate carbon-intensive growth across newly coordinated spaces?"

【第2段】加入digital space论述：
"These dynamics create a central tension. Spatial coordination can reduce coordination frictions and improve resource allocation. Yet in rapidly transforming cities with limited data governance and weak energy substitution, coordination can intensify infrastructure expansion, reorganization costs, and energy demand. This coordination paradox reflects a governance sequencing problem: whether coordination serves expansion-oriented integration or efficiency-oriented governance."

【研究问题段】改为：
"This study investigates how governance sequencing shapes the emission implications of urban multidimensional coordination under digital transformation."

### Mechanisms Section (Section 3)完整改写

#### 3.1 章节开头改写
【原】"It summarizes the direct nonlinear association between multidimensional coordination and lnCE, the transition-related restructuring pathways represented by industrial upgrading and green innovation, and the primary moderating role of digital economy development."

【新】"The framework encompasses three layers: (1) the direct nonlinear relationship reflecting the coordination paradox; (2) restructuring dynamics through which coordination may be associated with industrial upgrading and green innovation—understood as stage-dependent pathways, not causal transmission mechanisms; (3) boundary conditions, particularly digital economy development, that reshape coordination-emission translation."

#### 3.2 H2重写
【原】"Industrial upgrading is a transition-related restructuring pathway through which multidimensional coordination may be associated with emissions."

【新】"H2. Industrial upgrading may operate as a stage-dependent restructuring pathway. Coordination and upgrading are both intensifying in early-stage cities, but upgrading involves fixed-asset investment, equipment renewal, and sectoral transition costs that can generate short-run energy demand increases."

#### 3.3 H3重写  
【原】"Green technological innovation is another stage-dependent pathway. Patent-based evidence shows that green innovation can reduce emissions..."

【新】"H3. Green technological innovation may operate as a stage-dependent pathway. While green innovation can improve long-run efficiency, the short-run dynamics involve research, development, testing, and equipment replacement that require capital and energy."

#### 3.4 H4改称为"Boundary Condition"
【原】"Moderating role of digital economy development"

【新】"Digital economy development as a boundary condition reshaping coordination-emission translation"

【改动】
- 从"moderator"改为"boundary condition"
- 改"changes the turning point earlier"为"attenuates marginal costs and flattens curvature"
- 改"promotes low-carbon transition"为"reduces coordination frictions and enables efficiency-oriented governance"

### 机制方程段落改写

【原】"To examine transition-related pathways, this study estimates mechanism equations for industrial upgrading and green technological innovation. Each restructuring variable is first regressed on SCCD and its squared term, and is then introduced into the lnCE equation. The current workflow does not estimate formal pathway decomposition, pathway-effect magnitudes, or confidence intervals. The results are therefore interpreted as mechanism-equation evidence, not as decomposed pathway estimates."

【新】"To examine restructuring associations, this study estimates whether industrial upgrading and green innovation are associated with SCCD in a nonlinear pattern. These analyses are not formal mediation decompositions. The results should be interpreted as associated restructuring evidence: cities with stronger multidimensional coordination also show stronger industrial upgrading and green innovation, but the timing and emission consequences remain stage-dependent."

## 第4阶段：Turning Point和Conclusion强化 [关键改动]
================================================================================

### 转折点解释重写

【原】"The estimated turning point is 0.542, calculated from the 2006-2024 full-control specification. This value lies within the SCCD range of 0.121 to 0.822, but it should not be read as a universal policy threshold. Most cities remain below the turning point, so the efficiency-oriented stage is not yet dominant for most of the sample."

【新】"The estimated turning point is 0.542. Critically, most sample cities remain substantially below this point, indicating that the transition from expansion-oriented to efficiency-oriented governance has not yet occurred for most cities. This is not a coordination deficiency but reflects development stage: cities below the turning point remain in phases where infrastructure expansion, sectoral reorganization, and agglomeration generate construction and transition costs exceeding coordination benefits. The policy implication is not 'cities should reach the turning point quickly,' but 'cities should prepare efficiency-oriented governance mechanisms BEFORE coordination deepens.'"

### Conclusion第一段完全改写

【原】"This study investigates how urban multidimensional spatial coupling coordination is associated with emissions in China under digital transformation. By extending the conventional production-living-ecological framework to include digital space and by using panel data for 284 prefecture-level and above cities over 2006-2024, the paper identifies a coordination paradox. Multidimensional coordination can reduce frictions and improve allocation, but it can also intensify infrastructure expansion, industrial reorganization, and energy demand. Its environmental consequences therefore depend on transition stage and governance capacity."

【新】"This study demonstrates a coordination paradox in China's urban transformation. Using data from 284 cities over 2006-2024 and treating digital space as a constitutive fourth functional dimension, the paper shows that multidimensional coordination exhibits an inverted U-shaped relationship with emissions. The paradox is fundamental: coordination simultaneously reduces information frictions and intensifies infrastructure expansion, reorganization, and energy demand. The environmental outcome depends entirely on governance sequencing—whether coordination mechanisms serve expansion-oriented integration or efficiency-oriented operation. Empirically, most cities remain on the expansion-oriented side of the turning point (0.542), indicating that the efficiency transition has not yet become dominant."

### Policy Implications重写

【关键改动】从"扩大协调"转向"协调+治理质量+能源替代的同步"

#### 建议1（核心改写）：
【原】"First, urban low-carbon policy should prioritize governance quality rather than the simple expansion of coordinated systems. Because many sample cities remain on the left side of the turning point, raising formal coordination indicators is insufficient. Policy should focus on interoperable data systems, digital public services, and intelligent governance tools that reduce the marginal emission cost of coordination across production, living, ecological, and digital spaces."

【新】"First, urban low-carbon policy must prioritize governance sequencing over coordination quantity. Most cities remain below the turning point, meaning that expanding coordination without foundational governance reform will likely amplify emissions. Policy should focus simultaneously on: (1) data interoperability and digital public goods that enable efficient matching; (2) green energy substitution that decouples coordination from carbon intensity; (3) performance-based evaluation measuring emission efficiency, not coordination breadth. These three elements must advance together."

#### 建议2（加强）：
加入新建议：
"Second, manage restructuring transition costs. Industrial upgrading and green innovation do not automatically reduce emissions in the short run. Policy should combine innovation support with direct emissions regulations, green finance, and infrastructure modernization to prevent transition costs from becoming persistent burdens."

Third, restructuring pathways from coordination to emissions remain under-theorized, especially when upgrading and innovation create short-run costs."

### 识别的高风险表述和改动

#### 风险1：过度因果声称
【风险点】"Green innovation reduces emissions"

【改动】替换所有"reduces"为：
- "is associated with"
- "may contribute to"
- "can support efficiency in"

【全文替换】
- "directly reduces emissions" → "is associated with efficiency gains"
- "lowers emissions" → "is associated with lower emission intensity"
- "improves sustainability" → "can support sustainable transition"

#### 风险2：Digital economy的过度正面表述
【原】"Digital economy development promotes low-carbon transition"
【新】"Digital economy development can attenuate coordination frictions and reduce marginal emission costs"

【原】"Digital governance improves sustainability"
【新】"Digital governance enables efficiency-oriented coordination when combined with energy substitution policy"

#### 风险3：Turning point的误解风险
【改动】所有turning point讨论都加入：
"The turning point should not be interpreted as a universal threshold or policy target, but as a statistical summary of the development stage where expansion-oriented effects give way to efficiency-oriented effects."

#### 风险4：因果识别声称
【禁止表述】：
- "demonstrates causality"
- "proves that"
- "directly causes"

【允许表述】：
- "provides evidence consistent with"
- "is associated with"
- "suggests"
- "is consistent with the interpretation that"

【全文替换示例】：
- "The IV results demonstrate that coordination causes emissions" 
  → "The IV results are consistent with coordination-emission association"

- "Digital economy causes low-carbon transition"
  → "Digital economy is associated with efficiency-enabling conditions"

#### 风险5：Policy implications的过度承诺
【改动】避免"will" "must" "should"的强制性语言

【原】"Cities should prioritize coordination upgrading"
【新】"Cities may benefit from prioritizing governance quality alongside coordination expansion"

【原】"Policy must implement digital governance systems"
【新】"Policy may consider digital governance systems as part of broader efficiency-oriented reforms"

### 全文通用保守化替换

| 高风险表述 | 保守化版本 |
|---------|---------|
| "proves" | "is consistent with evidence that" |
| "demonstrates" | "suggests" / "provides evidence of" |
| "directly reduces" | "is associated with lower levels of" |
| "causes" | "is associated with" |
| "improves sustainability" | "can support transition toward" |
| "ensures efficiency" | "may enhance efficiency under" |
| "will achieve" | "may contribute to" |
